The Atelier

Fire-kissed craft, plated for couture palates.

Atelier Ember was born inside a test kitchen and quickly earned cult status for its slow browned brioche, single-origin cheeses, and design forward venues.

We approach burgers the way couturiers approach garments: every stitch matters. Expect hand-painted sauces, mezzanine lighting, playlists curated by vinyl sommeliers, and service choreography timed to each course.
